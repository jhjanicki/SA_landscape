Tracked annual climate finance reached R131 billion p.a. on average in 2019 – 2021, an all-time high, but still far from the average annual estimated needs of R334 – R535 billion p.a.

Although we saw an increase in climate finance tracked...

...it still needs to increase by at least three to fivefold to achieve the country’s climate objectives. South Africa currently requires on average ~R334 billion p.a. and ~R535 billion p.a. to meet net zero by 2050 and NDC by 2030, respectively.
